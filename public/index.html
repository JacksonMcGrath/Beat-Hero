      Beat Hero is a virtual instrument built to look and function like a beat pad. Hip hop producers such as J Dilla and Pete Rock made beat pads famous in the early 90s by producing whole records with just this instrument.

      The idea behind the instrument is that each pad is loaded with a unique audio sample to create a complete "drum kit". The user hits the pads to trigger the samples and can create whole songs by layering tracks ontop of eachother.

      This video breaks down the history behind beat pads and the craft involved in chopping, manipulating, and constructing songs. It also highlights how J Dilla changed the game with his creative use of the machine.

      Web Audio API

      Beat Hero was built using the Web Audio API to handle the audio sequencing and playback. The API processes audio with accurate timing and low latency which allowed me to sequence a whole beat without delay or distortion. The timing was an important factor in creating a functional game with such a strong need for accuracy in playback and measurement

      How to Play

      Keyboard

      By pressing CAPS LOCK you can use the keyboard to play each pad, and you can turn on the 'key guide' under the display to get situated with what key triggers what pad.

      Touch

      You can use a touch interface to play like you would a real beat pad, just tapping the pads. This allows for a more realistic experience and better practice for the real thing.

      Gameplay

      First, use the "Preview Round" button to hear the beat that you will attempt to recreate and practice along until you have the hang of it.

      Second, press the "Play Round" button to initiate the round. The round begins with a four beat count in before the beat begins playing. Hit the right pad on time and you will be rewarded with points.

      200 pts for a Perfect hit
      125 pts for a Great hit
      75 pts for a Good hit
      -100 pts for a Miss

      Medals

      After the round you, if you have enough points you will be awarded a metal and a chance to join the live leaderboard. If you do not have a passing score you can try again. The metals are awarded as follows.

      Bronze

      1800 pts

      Silver

      2400 pts

      Gold

      3000 pts
